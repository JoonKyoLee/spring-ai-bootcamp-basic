# Return & Refund Policy v1

> ⚠️ This document is deprecated. Please refer to the current policy (return-policy-v3.md).

## Standard Return Window

All products may be returned within **7 calendar days** of delivery, provided the item is
unopened and in original packaging.

### Non-Returnable Items

- Clearance items
- Digital downloads
- Personalized items
- Food and beverages

## Handling Fee

Non-defect returns incur a handling fee of **5,000 won** deducted from the refund.

## Refund Timeline

Credit card refunds: 5–7 business days.
Bank transfer refunds: up to 10 business days.

## Marketplace Items

Marketplace items follow Cholog's standard 7-day return policy uniformly.
(Note: This was changed in v2 and v3 — Marketplace items now follow seller-specific policies.)
