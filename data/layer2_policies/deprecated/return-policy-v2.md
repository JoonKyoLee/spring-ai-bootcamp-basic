# Return & Refund Policy v2

> ⚠️ This document is deprecated. Please refer to the current policy (return-policy-v3.md).

## Standard Return Window

All products may be returned within **10 calendar days** of delivery. The item must be
unused with original packaging intact.

### Exceptions

| Category | Return Window |
|----------|--------------|
| Electronics | 7 days |
| Food & Beverages | Not returnable |
| Cosmetics | 7 days, must be unused |
| Marketplace Items | Per seller policy (3–14 days) |

## Handling Fee

Non-defect returns incur a handling fee of **3,000 won** deducted from the refund.
(Same as current v3 — but the return window differs.)

## Refund Timeline

Credit card refunds: 3–5 business days after inspection.
Kakao Pay / Naver Pay: Typically instant.
Bank transfer: Up to 7 business days.
