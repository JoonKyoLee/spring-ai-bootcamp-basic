# Standard Shipping Policy v3 (2023)

> ⚠️ This document is deprecated. Free shipping threshold changed to 20,000 won effective
> 2024-01-01. Refer to the current shipping-standard.md.

## Shipping Tiers (2023)

| Tier | Fee | Delivery Time |
|------|-----|---------------|
| Economy | 2,500 won | 2–4 business days |
| Priority | 5,000 won | Next business day by noon |
| Same-Day | 8,000 won | Same day (Seoul/Gyeonggi) |

## Free Shipping Threshold (2023)

- **Standard members**: Economy shipping free on orders ≥ **30,000 won**
- **Plus members**: Economy shipping free on orders ≥ **15,000 won**
- **VIP members**: Priority shipping free on all orders

(Note: As of 2024-01-01, Standard member threshold was reduced to 20,000 won and Plus
members now receive free Economy shipping on ALL orders with no minimum.)

## Dispatch Cutoff

Orders confirmed before **2 PM KST** (changed to 1 PM KST in 2024).
