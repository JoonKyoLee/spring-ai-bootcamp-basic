# Loyalty Point Earning Rules v1 (Legacy)

> ⚠️ This document is deprecated. Point rates were revised significantly in June 2023.
> Refer to point-earning-rules.md (v3) for current rates.

## Legacy Earning Rates (Pre-June 2023)

| Membership Tier | Earning Rate |
|----------------|--------------|
| Standard | **3%** of order subtotal |
| Gold | **5%** of order subtotal |
| VIP | **7%** of order subtotal |

(Note: The membership tiers were renamed and restructured in 2023. "Gold" became "Plus".
Earning rates were reduced significantly to align with industry standards.)

## Legacy Point Expiry

Points expired **24 months** from the date earned (now reduced to 12 months).

## Legacy Redemption

Minimum redemption was **500 points** (now increased to 1,000 points).
